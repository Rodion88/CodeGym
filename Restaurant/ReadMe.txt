Restaurant menu in Java

Program to automate what happens in a restaurant.

The task is this - the director of the restaurant wants to:

there was a tablet on each table through which one could place an order;
while the order is being prepared, ads were shown on the tablet;
at the end of the working day there was an opportunity to look at various statistics: 
a) the workload of the cook;
b) the amount of revenue for orders; 
c) the amount of revenue for ad impressions.
